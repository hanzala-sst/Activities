Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs and milk, mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Let it cool before serving.

Enjoy your homemade chocolate cake!
